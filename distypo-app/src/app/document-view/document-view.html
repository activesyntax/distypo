DisTypo is a typography corrector. You paste in your text, and the application points out the small mechanical mistakes that spell-checkers ignore — the wrong dash, the missing space, the straight quote in a paragraph of curly ones. It does not rewrite your prose. It only fixes what is unambiguously wrong.

The issues DisTypo currently detects include the following.

Sentences beginning with a lowercase letter:

the meeting starts at noon. → The meeting starts at noon.

Missing space after punctuation:

noon.We started. → noon. We started.

Double spaces between words:

the quick  brown fox → the quick brown fox

Space before punctuation:

Are you serious ? → Are you serious?

Straight quotes instead of typographic ones:

"hello," she said → “hello,” she said

Hyphens used in place of dashes:

pages 12-18 → pages 12–18

she paused - then continued → she paused — then continued

Three periods instead of an ellipsis:

and then...nothing → and then… nothing

Inconsistent spacing around parentheses:

result( see figure 3 )is clear → result (see figure 3) is clear

None of these mistakes are serious on their own. What makes them worth fixing is that they accumulate. DisTypo takes that residue out of your text so the words can do their job.
