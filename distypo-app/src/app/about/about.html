DisTypo is a typography corrector. You paste in your text, and the application points out the small mechanical mistakes that spell-checkers ignore — the wrong dash, the missing space, the straight quote in a paragraph of curly ones.

It does not rewrite your prose. It only fixes what is unambiguously wrong. Each correction is shown inline, with the original and the suggestion side by side, so you stay in control of every change.
